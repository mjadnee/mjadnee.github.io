Hello! My name is Maren and I am a physics and mathematics student at the University of Maine.
I like statistics, geometry, and physics.
I hope to better understand the nature of space and time by using advanced statistical methods to analyze data from experiments neutrino, gravitational wave, and dark matter detection.

I am also very involved with art and artists communities in Kyoto, Tokyo, and New York City. I have a small collection of artworks inspired by my research which I am compiling into a portfolio.
If you are interested in seeing it or collaborating on an art project, please reach out to me at maren.adnee@gmail.com.
